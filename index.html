Olivia Rodrigo

Olivia Rodrigo is an American singer-songwriter and actress who rose to fame with her breakout single "drivers license" in 2021. Born on February 20, 2003, in Murrieta, California, Olivia developed a passion for music and acting at a young age. She gained early recognition through her roles on Disney shows like Bizaardvark and High School Musical: The Musical: The Series, but it was her transition into the music industry that truly catapulted her into the global spotlight.
