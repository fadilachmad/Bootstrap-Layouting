Olivia Rodrigo

Olivia Rodrigo is an American singer-songwriter and actress who rose to fame with her breakout single "drivers license" in 2021. Born on February 20, 2003, in Murrieta, California, Olivia developed a passion for music and acting at a young age. She gained early recognition through her roles on Disney shows like Bizaardvark and High School Musical: The Musical: The Series, but it was her transition into the music industry that truly catapulted her into the global spotlight.

Olivia Rodrigo first gained public attention through her role as Paige in the Disney Channel series Bizaardvark and as Nini in High School Musical: The Musical: The Series. Her debut single, "Drivers License," released in 2021, quickly went viral globally, topping the charts in multiple countries. Olivia has cited musicians like Taylor Swift and Lorde as major influences, shaping her emotional and deep lyric-writing style. Besides being a singer, Olivia is also known as an excellent songwriter with the ability to play both guitar and piano. Interestingly, Olivia began writing songs at the age of 12, with much of her work inspired by her personal experiences.

Olivia Rodrigo has earned numerous prestigious awards since her debut in the music industry. In 2022, she won three Grammy Awards, including Best New Artist, Best Pop Vocal Album for her album SOUR, and Best Pop Solo Performance for the song "Drivers License." At the 2022 Billboard Music Awards, Olivia took home several awards, including Top New Artist and Top Hot 100 Artist. Additionally, at the 2021 MTV Video Music Awards (VMA), Olivia was named Best New Artist and won Push Performance of the Year. "Drivers License" even broke a Guinness World Record as the first song by a female artist to be the most streamed on Spotify in one week.

Olivia Rodrigo has had several exciting musical collaborations. On her album SOUR, Olivia worked with Taylor Swift on the song "1 Step Forward, 3 Steps Back," which was inspired by Swift's song "New Year's Day." Music producer Dan Nigro was also one of Olivia's closest collaborators, working on nearly all the songs on SOUR, including "Drivers License" and "Good 4 U." In the High School Musical: The Musical: The Series project, Olivia collaborated with actor and singer Joshua Bassett, particularly on the song "Just for a Moment." Her friendship with musician Conan Gray further adds to Olivia's list of collaborations, with the two often sharing moments together in support of Taylor Swift's music.
